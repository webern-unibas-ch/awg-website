The Anton Webern Gesamtausgabe’s most important cooperation partners:

Swiss National Data & Service Center for the Humanities (DaSCH)

Digital Humanities Lab, Universität Basel

Österreichische Akademie der Wissenschaften

Paul Sacher Stiftung, Basel

Universal Edition, Wien

In addition, there are numerous contacts and substantive exchange with related edition projects (Alban Berg, Arnold Schönberg, Briefwechsel der Wiener Schule), numerous archives, as well as with the international Webern research community. In the area of Digital Humanities, there are also contacts to important communities such as DHd or DHOxSS, to the Music Encoding Community (MEI, ViFE/Edirom) as well as to music-related Semantic Web projects (DOREMUS, MELD, TROMPA).
