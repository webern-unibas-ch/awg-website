The Anton Webern Gesamtausgabe is part of a network which forms present day scientific discourse on Anton Webern, whether through conferences and workshops, the series of publication Webern-Studien or, at irregular intervals, with lectures on the composer. The collaborators of the AWG, moreover, regularly contribute scientific papers and publications on current research questions. Furthermore, the AWG actively encourages cooperation with interpreters.
