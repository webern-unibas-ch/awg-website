The Anton Webern Lectures are a lecture series on Webern and his milieu given at irregular intervals by internationally renowned musicologists. These lectures are aimed at scholars, students, and music enthusiasts. Past events:
  • Rudolf Stephan (Berlin): Anton Webern und seine Zeit, auditorium of the Department of Musicology of the University of Basel, October 19, 2010, at 18.30.
  • Elmar Budde (Kandern): Extase und Askese oder Ohnmacht der Töne – über Anton Webern, auditorium of the Department of Musicology of the University of Basel, October 20, 2011, at 19.00.
  • Christian Martin Schmidt (Berlin): Hilft die Reihenanalyse bei der Edition von Zwölftonwerken Weberns?, auditorium of the Department of Musicology of the University of Basel, May 20, 2014, at 18.15.
  • Horst Weber (Berlin): Webern und die Tradition des Kanons, auditorium of the Department of Musicology of the University of Basel, April 28, 2015, at 18.15.
  • Simone Hohmaier (Berlin): Die Korrespondenz zwischen Anton Webern und Alban Berg – Dokument einer wundersamen Freundschaft, auditorium of the Department of Musicology of the University of Basel, November 27, 2018, at 18.15.
  • Sebastian Wedler (Oxford): Auf der Sucher nach Weberns Frühstil, auditorium of the Department of Musicology of the University of Basel, November 23, 2021, at 18.15
