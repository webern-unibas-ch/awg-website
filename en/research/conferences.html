The AWG organizes international as well as interdisciplinary conferences and workshops dealing with specific aspects of Webern’s oeuvre. Past events:

  • Der junge Webern (The young Webern)
    1. Künstlerische Orientierungen in Wien nach 1900, Austrian Academy of Sciences, Vienna, March 5–6, 2012.
    2. Texte und Kontexte, Department of Musicology of the University of Basel, May 31 to June 1, 2012. [More]
  • webern@segantini
    1. Interdisciplinary conference and concert focusing on Anton Webern's String Quartet (1905) and Giovanni Segantini’s ‚Alpentriptychon‘, Segantini-Museum, St. Moritz, April 7, 2014. [More]
      The conference concluded with a concert by the Pellegrini Quartet of Webern’s String Quartet (M 79) in the edition so far prepared by the AWG.
    2. Workshop with Markus Böggemann (Kassel) in collaboration with the NCCR Iconic Criticism eikones, Department of Musicology of the University of Basel, May 12, 2014.
