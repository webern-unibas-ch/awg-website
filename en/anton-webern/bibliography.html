On the DaSCH Service Platform (DSP), the Anton Webern Complete Edition is compiling a bibliography with original writings of the composer as well as research literature related to Anton Webern. It includes articles in journals and anthologies, monographs, university theses and encyclopaedia entries. This bibliographical database thus offers a comprehensive overview of the international scholarly work on Anton Webern from 1907 to the present day and is constantly being expanded.
