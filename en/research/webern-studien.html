The Webern-Studien (supplements to the Anton Webern Gesamtausgabe) are a series instituted in 2012 that contributes to the on-going reflection on Webern, the man and his work, and they serve as media for the publication of accompanying material (e.g. diaries or letters). To date, the following volumes have been published:
  • Wechselnde Erscheinung. Sechs Perspektiven auf Anton Weberns sechste Bagatelle, ed. Simon Obert, Vienna: Lafite, 2012 (Webern-Studien 1). [Contents and Introduction (PDF)]
  • Der junge Webern. Künstlerische Orientierungen in Wien nach 1900, ed. Monika Kröpfl and Simon Obert, Vienna: Lafite, 2015 (Webern-Studien 2a). [Contents and Preface (PDF)]
  • Der junge Webern. Texte und Kontexte, ed. Thomas Ahrend and Matthias Schmidt, Vienna: Lafite, 2015 (Webern-Studien 2b). [Contents and Preface (PDF)]
  • Webern-Philologien, ed. Thomas Ahrend and Matthias Schmidt, Vienna: Lafite, 2016 (Webern-Studien 3). [Contents and Preface (PDF)] [Complete Volume (PDF, 7 MB) ]
  • Neue Perspektiven. Anton Webern und das Komponieren im 20. Jahrhundert, ed. Pietro Cavallotti, Simon Obert and Rainer Schmusch, Wien: Lafite, 2019 (Webern-Studien 4). [Contents and Preface (PDF)]
  • Anton Webern, Briefwechsel mit der Universal-Edition, hg. von Julia Bungardt, Wien: Lafite, 2020 (Webern-Studien 5). [Contents and Preface (PDF)]

The Webern-Studien were published by Lafite, Vienna, until 2020. edition text + kritik will take over the previously published volumes from 2024 and will manage the series in future.
